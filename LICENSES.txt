# Asset Attribution & Licenses

This document outlines the licensing and attribution requirements for all external data and image assets integrated into HitConfirm.

All frame data (startup, active, recovery, advantage, damage, guard) displayed in the Strategy Hub and Character Details pages are fetched dynamically from the **Dustloop Wiki Cargo API** (https://www.dustloop.com/).

## 3. Local Custom Character Portraits
To maintain a high-quality, cohesive dark neon aesthetic, the core character portraits (`Sol_Badguy.png`, `Ryu.png`, `Kazuya.png`, `Mario.png` under `/src/images/characters/`) were custom illustrated using the Google DeepMind AI agent, staying strictly faithful to the original, official visual designs of the copyright holders.
